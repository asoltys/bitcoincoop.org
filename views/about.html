About Us

We're a voluntary and inclusive association of individuals united by a shared interest in Bitcoin, a new and improved kind of money and financial system. We organize presentations, discussions, pub nights, parties, and other creative outings to community of people and businesses interested in learning about and creating a new economy.

We help businesses accept Bitcoin payments in their brick and mortar locations and online. We provide one-on-one tutoring and consultation to help people learn how Bitcoin works.

As a registered co-operative association in the province of British Columbia, we have an open membership structure and adhere to the Co-op Principles. Members can vote on resolutions at our meetings, participate in elections of our board of directors and have a say in how we prioritize our projects and finances. Come to our next meeting or event to get to know us and learn how you can get involved!
