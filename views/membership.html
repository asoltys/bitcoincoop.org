Membership

Membership is open to any person or organization who shares our values and wants to participate in our activities, subject to the approval of our democratically elected Board of Directors. To become a member, fill out the following form and send payment to the address provided. The fee is $20, payable in Bitcoin.

After signing up, you'll be given a member number and recorded in our register. We'll invite you to attend our Annual General Meetings where you'll be able to vote on resolutions as well as nominate and elect directors. You can find more information about co-operatives by visiting the British Columbia Co-operative Association and reading the BC Co-operative Association Act. You can also read our Bylaws and Memorandum of Agreement.
