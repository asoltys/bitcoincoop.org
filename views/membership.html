Membership

Membership in our co-operative is open to any person or organization, subject to the approval of our directors. To become a member, fill out the following form and send a payment of $20 to the address provided.

Once approved, you will be given a member number and recorded in our membership registry. We'll invite you to our meetings where you'll be able to vote on resolutions and elect directors of the co-op. Please read our bylaws and memorandum of agreement to learn more about how we operate and what membership entails.
