CoinFest 2015, Vancouver

CoinFest is a decentralized, open source, 3-day celebration of cryptocurrency. The first CoinFest was held in Vancouver in 2013; it later spread to cities across Canada as a simultaneous convention. Now CoinFest is a holiday celebrated around the world, with 25 cities gearing up to incentivize adoption and educate the public.

CoinFest in Vancouver will last all 3 days, and continue to uphold the traditions of free attendance and the no-fiat rule. February 22 will once again be the conference, and February 21 will be a decentralized variety of events around downtown. February 20 will hold an Intro to Bitcoin for the uninitiated; please check out all of our events and sponsors!

Don't miss out on additional attractions like a new Bitcoin food truck and bring receipts from Steamrollers or Bestie to redeem for poker chips! If you're looking for a place to say, the Sandman Hotel accepts bitcoins, and we can arrange for a deal. For details on what's going on in other cities around the world, please see CoinFest.org.
